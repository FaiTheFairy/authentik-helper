# Membership

The Membership tables let you move people between Guests and Members.

## Promote a guest

1. Open the Guests table.
2. Find a user and click Promote to Member.
3. If SMTP is configured, they receive a promotion email.

## Demote a member

1. Open the Members table.
2. Find a user and click Demote to Guest.

## Bulk actions

1. Select multiple rows.
2. Use Bulk Promote or Bulk Demote.
3. The status area reports how many succeeded and failed.
